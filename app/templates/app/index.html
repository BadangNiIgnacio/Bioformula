About Badang ni Ignacio

Badang ni Ignacio is run by Christian Life Community (CLC) and is accredited by Agricultural Training Institute (ATI) as a learning site for agriculture. It is an 11-hectare farm engaging both in plants and animals and is complete with facilities to provide students all the trainings they need in agriculture. To learn and adopt topics on agriculture and crop science, organic and sustainable farming, swine farming, poultry farming, making of organic fertilizers, crop protection and management.

Badang ni Ignacio is known for its Prayer Labyrinth field, a serene place where individuals can reflect and pray as they walk in a circular path. It provides a peaceful environment for meditation and deep contemplation. Visitors often find solace and spiritual renewal in this sacred space.

Here at Badang ni Ignacio, the farm is renowned for its commitment to natural farming, employing well-known and traditional methods to cultivate plants naturally and organically. They use concocted organic fertilizers and pesticides to promote healthier plant growth. These practices not only enhance crop yield but also ensure environmentally sustainable farming.

This is one of the highlights of Badang ni Ignacio, where anyone, including visitors, can swim or take a refreshing bath. The natural spring water, sourced from its pristine environment such as caves and surrounded by trees, accompanied by the soothing sounds of birds and the serene, peaceful atmosphere, creates a profoundly positive impact, helping everyone feel rejuvenated.

The Communities of Practice (CoP) is an activity held in Badang ni Ignacio, where students from different schools can learn and create organic concoctions used in making organic fertilizers and pesticides. It serves as a platform for collaborative learning and hands-on experience in organic farming practices. Participants are encouraged to share knowledge and techniques that contribute to sustainable agriculture. The initiative also aims to promote environmental awareness and the practical application of eco-friendly solutions in farming.
